Weekly Giveaways

 

Oh snap! Those delicious pictures of 4 Rivers BBQ that you’ve been posting may score you some free swag this summer. From June 3 – August 3, celebrate an Endless Summer of BBQ with us by using #4Rsummer when you post on social media. We’ll be giving away weekly prizes ranging from free sandwiches to theme park tickets to free BBQ for an entire year to followers who use our summer hashtag or engage with us on social media. Follow along each week on Facebook, Instagram, or Twitter as we announce ways you can win!

 Events

 

Memorial Day
On Memorial Day, Monday, May 27 the entire 4 Rivers family is proud to offer one free Classic Sandwich at all Smokehouse locations to active military, veterans, and first responders. Thank you for your service!

National Brisket Day
One of the tastiest days of the year, National Brisket Day, is on Tuesday, May 28. To celebrate, each Smokehouse will be giving out a free Brisket Sandwich voucher (for a return visit) to the first 100 guests that day. Also, be sure to follow 4 Rivers Smokehouse’s social media accounts on National Brisket Day for real-time giveaways all day long!

4th of July
Last year was so delicious, we decided to all over again! For the second time ever, all Smokehouse locations, except Downtown Orlando, will be open on the 4th of July from 11 am to 5 pm. Also on July 4th, we invite all active duty military, veterans, and first responders to enjoy one free classic sandwich as our thank you for your service. All other days throughout the year, don’t forget that you receive a 10% discount at all Smokehouses with valid Military I.D.
